About Us

MANAGEMENT/ADMINISTRATION

RESOURCE PERSONS:


A Board of Directors and an Executive Working Committee govern the organization. An Executive Director who is directly responsible to the governing bodies oversees the day-to-day functioning of the two NEWLO centers in Grenada; the main centre at Palmiste and a subsidiary centre at Grenville. The staff comprises three coordinators, six Adolescent Development instructors, nineteen technical and vocational skills instructors, and six administrative staff members. A number of temporary volunteers from Grenada and abroad supplement the permanent staff.

About NEWLO

Mr. Lloyd Noel

The New Life Organization (NEWLO) is a skills training center located in Grenada in the West Indies. NEWLO has, for years, been dedicated to providing quality life and vocational skills training for disadvantaged youth, facilitating growth and development of the individual in relation to the family, the community, and the nation. The trainees range in age from 17 to 24 years. During the academic year, NEWLO trainees attend classes and training sessions at one of the two centers in Grenada, namely: Palmiste and Grenville (St. Andrew’s Life Centre). NEWLO relies on contributions, donations, subvention, and sponsorships from the Roman Catholic Church (both home and abroad), the Government of Grenada, companies in the private sector, individuals, and fund-raising efforts for its operation.
