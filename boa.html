Basic Office Administration

The Office Skills prepares trainees for clerical and/or secretarial duties. Their training focuses mainly on computer literacy, typing, document creation and editing; students also undergo training in basic office procedures, decorum, and etiquette, communication skills, advanced literacy and numeracy, and customer service. Office skills trainees go on to apply their skills in government or the private sector.
