The New Life Organization (NEWLO) is a skills training center located in Grenada in the West Indies. NEWLO has, for years, been dedicated to providing quality life and vocational skills training for disadvantaged youth, facilitating growth and development of the individual in relation to the family, the community, and the nation. The trainees range in age from 17 to 24 years.
